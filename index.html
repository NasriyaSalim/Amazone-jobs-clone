Devin C.

Software Development Engineer

Devin came to Seattle from Columbus, Ohio soon after graduating college. Having a background in
hard sciences and finance gives him a diverse perspective and allows him to focus on delivering
customer obsessed solutions. Shortly after joining Amazon, Devin was able to contribute to several
high-impact computer vision projects with his team. Devin is now working on streamlining the
process to train machine learning models.

Devin’s favorite part about Amazon is the level of autonomy that is given to employees.“Being at
Amazon is really all about what you make of it. You are the one in control of your career here.”

Growing up in the Midwest, he was always immersed in the arts and performed in choirs, theatre
groups, and concert symphonies. His experience in music has instilled a drive to constantly self-
improve and strive for excellence in his personal and professional life. At home, he loves cooking
spicy foods, cruising on his longboard, and is an ardent Virtual Reality gamer.
